A testimony to the beginning of my work in graphic design began with my time as a Breakaway volunteer. I have the greatest honor to serve this ministry throughout my time in college and this semester I am on Creative Team making social media graphics and videoing for Instagram stories on Tuesday nights. Working with the Breakaway brand allows me to explore and grow my design skills and has only deepened my desire for creating.
